Welcome to Pacific Trails Resort

Immerse yourself in the tranquility of nature at Pacific Trails Resort, where our guests enjoy the ultimate in comfort and relaxation. Situated on the scenic California coast, our resort boasts luxurious yurts, breathtaking ocean views, and private access to miles of pristine trails.

Whether you're seeking a peaceful retreat to disconnect from the hustle of daily life or an adventurous getaway filled with hiking, kayaking, and star-gazing, Pacific Trails offers an unparalleled experience. Our facilities include a spa, outdoor adventure tours, and fine dining, all designed to harmonize with the natural environment.

Discover the beauty of Pacific Trails Resort, where luxury meets wilderness. Book your stay with us and find your paradise away from the world.
